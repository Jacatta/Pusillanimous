Abstract

I created this project to showcase my mobile development abilities. Within three weeks I had developed a mechanic that seemed to grab players interest. See Previous Index. I was able to build off this, focus my direction by thinking about my target audience, focus on polish and ease of use. With many revisions and 3 months of work 'in my free time' this is what I have to show.

Gameplay

The core game play mechanic has not changed much due to the positive reactions received from play testers. Further development is planned for the 2nd half of the level.

Design

I drew out the UI I imagined for the game. This included the character, background, and more.

Assets

Almost none of the original assets are still in use in the current version. Many placeholders have been gathered from google, as I go I am replacing them with my own work.
