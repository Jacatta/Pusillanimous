Abstract

I created this project to showcase my mobile development abilities. I wanted to create a quick prototype so that I could get feedback and change things accordingly. I started with a simple mechanic and immediatly began testing with users. After seeings that users prefered to play the game in a fast paced manner I began developing the UI and later mechanics around this idea. I created assets in Photoshop and am in the process of developing and refining the core gameplay. It has been 3 weeks and this is what I have to show.

Initial Gameplay

Originally I was developing a simple game of jumping from one ledge to another, After seeing the addictive reaction towards target tapping feature of my game, I decided to move forward with the idea of a quick tapping game.

Assets

Initially I created a blue blob of a character, but this evolved into a cute purple octopus. I also created backgrounds including the sky and the ground from an aerial perspective.
